Block Chain

Bitcoin’s rise is spectacular as the first decentralized digital cryptocurrency. It has managed to cause a major stir in the world economy today. However, the most exciting thing about Bitcoin isn’t Bitcoin. It’s the encryption technology behind Bitcoin, called blockchain. Today, the blockchain technology is widely adopted across industries. The demand for blockchain professionals is increasing rapidly, which reflects through the constantly increasing number of job posts on LinkedIn.

Star Expert Blockchain Specialist is a comprehensive course that primarily focuses on the blockchain technology and explains why a shift to digital currency matters. This course addresses the important questions about Bitcoin, such as: How does the blockchain technology work? What makes it different? How secure are Bitcoins? How to mine Bitcoins? This course would be a great source of information for students and professionals who would like to dive into the world of cryptocurrencies.

The SEBS program helps the learners acquire a fundamental understanding of the blockchain technology, blockchain for Bitcoins and other applications like setting up smart contracts. Further, this program explains how to set up Bitcoin wallets, transactions, and security of Bitcoins. The purpose of the program is to help the learners master the skills they need to establish a successful career as a blockchain developer in this dynamic space. The course deconstructs the blockchain technology and covers all the basic and advanced concepts including cryptocurrency, cryptography, Bitcoin ecosystem, Bitcoin design, Bitcoin mining, Bitcoin security, ALT Coins and DApps.
